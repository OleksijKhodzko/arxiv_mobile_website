About ArchivePapers

ArchivePapers is a Flutter-based mobile application that enhances your experience reading scientific papers from arXiv. Our app provides PDF-to-HTML conversion for improved readability on mobile devices while maintaining the original content integrity.

We offer free access to all arXiv papers in their original PDF format. Our optional conversion service transforms PDFs into mobile-friendly HTML format for a small fee to cover processing costs.
